The Department of Health in accordance with the Law 81 of 1912 and by virtue of the Executive Order 2021-037 is requesting information that allows to adequately understand the COVID-19 situation in Puerto Rico. The requested information in this document will be handled confidentially and will be used for Sara Alert electronic system registration.

* Every person traveling to Puerto Rico must complete this declaration.
* In the event of traveling with dependent minors, the minor's information shall and must be completed in the same adult form.
* If a minor is traveling without adult supervision to Puerto Rico, an adult in charge is the one who must complete the declaration, before taking the flight.
* In the event that a minor arrives to Puerto Rico without the completed declaration form, the protocol established for these cases will be activated.

Your collaboration is very important for keeping our community safe.

Thank you!
